About Us

MikrodTech provides professional networking services and supplies quality technology products. We install and support computers, networking equipment, CCTV/security systems, printers, power backups (UPS), POS systems, and gaming & productivity gear. Our focus is dependable installations, fast service, and premium products tailored for institutions and businesses.

Services & Products

Wired & wireless networks, structured cabling, switches, routers, and Wi-Fi planning for offices and campuses.

MikrodTech designs and installs secure wired & wireless networks. We handle structured cabling (Cat6/Cat7), routers, switches, and Wi-Fi planning tailored for schools, offices, and institutions. Our focus is speed, reliability, and scalability for future growth.

Desktops, laptops, and workstations for productivity and education — sales, setup, and maintenance.

We supply, install, and maintain desktops, laptops, and workstations for businesses and schools. Our team handles OS setup, software configuration, upgrades, and ongoing support to keep your teams productive.

Camera systems, DVR/NVR setup, remote viewing, and secure installations for facilities.

MikrodTech installs CCTV surveillance systems with DVR/NVR integration. We provide remote monitoring, secure setup, and reliable maintenance to protect your facilities, schools, and businesses.

Printer supply & maintenance, POS hardware setup and integration for retail or school environments.

From printers and photocopiers to POS hardware, we supply, install, and maintain solutions tailored to schools, offices, and retail businesses. Our team ensures smooth integration and reliable operation.

Power continuity solutions to keep critical systems running during outages.

We provide UPS systems and backup power solutions that protect against downtime and data loss. MikrodTech ensures your critical infrastructure stays online even during outages.

High-performance peripherals and components for gaming labs and productivity suites.

We supply and install high-performance keyboards, mice, monitors, and components for gaming labs, eSports setups, and productivity environments. Built for speed, comfort, and performance.
